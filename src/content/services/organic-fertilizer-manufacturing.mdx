Manufacturing organic fertilizers and coated granules with precision, we blend advanced technology with sustainable practices to produce high-quality, nutrient-rich products. Each batch is crafted to enhance soil health, maximize crop yields, and meet the rigorous standards of modern agriculture.

Our state-of-the-art facility is certified with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 standards, operating round-the-clock to produce sustainable agricultural inputs for domestic and international markets.

## Manufacturing Capabilities

J K Fertilizers possesses extensive, high-volume production capabilities across multiple units:

### Granule Manufacturing
- **Total Capacity**: 700 MT / Day
- **Infrastructure**: Unit 1 (100 MT/Day), Unit 2 (200 MT/Day), and Unit 3 (400 MT/Day).
- **Quality**: Uniform, dust-free granules engineered for mechanical field spreaders.

### Powder Manufacturing
- **Total Capacity**: 400 MT / Day
- **Infrastructure**: Unit 1 (200 MT/Day) and Unit 2 (200 MT/Day).
- **Quality**: Highly soluble and uniform powder formulations.

### Coating & Specialization Facilities
- **Granule Coating Base**: 100 MT / Day dedicated to preparing base granules for special treatments.
- **Finished Granule Coating**: 190 MT / Day across Unit 1 (30 MT/Day), Unit 2 (60 MT/Day), and Unit 3 (100 MT/Day).
- **Liquid Fertilizer Packing**: 15,000 Litres / Day capacity with automated filling and packaging lines.

## Why Choose Our Manufacturing?

- **Chemical-Free Production**: Purely organic and eco-friendly input materials that promote long-term soil vitality.
- **Customized Nutrient Blending**: Ability to incorporate NPK, Mycorrhiza, humic acids, and other bio-enhancers into custom formulations.
- **Rigorous Quality Control**: In-house laboratory testing at every stage—from raw material verification to finished batch assessment.
- **Logistical Scale**: Capacity to handle large bulk orders with rapid turnaround times, supported by prime transport access in Gujarat.
